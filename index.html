Sailing in Maldives!Cassie's birthday trip 2018!

"Maldives?! I asked her. Where on earth is the Maldives?!" My best friend since the 7th grade had gotten married and spent the honeymoon in Maldives. I listened as her eyes lit up while she described the white sands, and the multi-colored marine life. She smiled ear to ear as she talked of the friendly locals and how the Maldives was made up of 1000s of coral islands with blue lagoons and beautiful little villas with glass floors on stilts spread throughout the country. It was then and there, some 8 years ago that I decided I was going to celebrate my 40th birthday in the Maldives.

DAILY ITINERARYHighlights Of Our Journey

START END
DAY 1

Swim With Manta Rays at Banana Reef

Hulhumale Lagoon / Kohdhipparu

We arrive at the international airport of Male' by 2pm. We will then take a 20 min boat ride to Hulhumale' lagoon and board our Catamaran. After 30 min. sailing, we will arrive at Banana Reef (North Male' Atoll) a superb snorkeling site where we will certainly see manta rays. We will then set sail towards the Ari Atoll (3 hours) and spend the night at Kohdhipparu lagoon.

  • Tourist Attraction:
    Manta Rays
  • Best Buy of this Place:
    Handmade Wooden Accessories
  • Time Sailing:
    3 hours 30 minutes
  • Activity:
    Snorkeling
TOUR DELIGHT :
Dinner with the locals
DAY 2

Snorkel With Sea Turtles at Rasdhoo

Diving/ Fishing

After 4hours of sailing we will arrive at Rasdhoo, a local island where we can enjoy the local life style while having tea, coffee or cold drinks at the local coffee shops located in front of the port. After snorkeling in the lagoon and exploring the area we will spend the night in the lagoon of Rasdhoo.

  • Tourist Attraction:
    Diving

DAY 3

Spend The Night In Fesdhoo Lagoon

Genburugau / Dhigga

We sail for 2h30mins and arrive at another beautiful snorkeling site named Genburugau. After a 1h45mins sailing lunch, we visit Dhigga, a reef with an abundance of marine life and corals. We sail for another 45mins to spend the night in the beautiful blue Lagoon of Fesdhoo.

  • Tourist Attraction:

DAY 4

Explore Enboodhoo

Moofushi / Rahddhiggaa

We set sail for 2 hours to arrive at the lagoon of Moofushi. After 45mins sailing, we arrive at the uninhabited island Enboodhoo where we will explore the island’s natural beauty by taking a tour around the island. We can snorkel if we choose so, or just relax on the beach. We sail for 45mins to the picnic island Rahddhiggaa where we can enjoy a picnic at the beach or snorkel with colorful fish and marine life in the surrounding lagoon. The night will be spent in the lagoon of Rahddhiggaa.

  • Tourist Attraction:
    Uninhabited Island Enboodhoo
